Who are we?

Mentored Research is an financial education firm established by alumni of BITS Pilani, India; providing career growth-oriented finance programs online for students, young professionals and other interested enthusiasts. Our aim is to enable interested participants at any location gain access to top-quality course content and support systems, in order to launch their personal or professional journey in the lucrative world of finance.

What do we do?

Since 2010, Mentored Research has provided courses on Equity Research, Risk Management and Technical Analysis to students in top universities and colleges like IITs, BITS, NITs, Delhi University, LSR, etc and also to enthusiasts from all corners of the country including Mumbai, Pune, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Delhi and Chennai. Our programs focus on both theoretical and practical learning, emphasizing on hands-on approach as a highly effective method to understand the nuances of finance. We carefully nurture the spirit of inquisitiveness and research within the students, encouraging and daring them to reach new heights. Our alumni are now highly successful industry professionals, bringing glory to themselves and to Mentored Research.

Our Programs​

Mentored Research currently provides the following programs: Equity Research - focused on understanding Excel basics, fundamental analysis, ratio analysis, valuation and​ pitching of a real life company stock; Risk Management - focused on giving a solid introduction to various areas of financial risk which corporations face, and quantifying the same; Technical Analysis - focused on gaining an insight into the numerical and chart-based indicators and theories for tracking stock prices and analyzing expected future prices. We have had numerous editions of these till now, Equity Research being our oldest program.
