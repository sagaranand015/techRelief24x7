MENTORED-RESEARCH

Mentored-Research is an organization spearheaded by alumni of BITS Pilani University, India, working to increase access to educational resources to determined students. We visualize a world where skilled and experienced individuals, with diverse expertise, educate ambitious students. Founded in 2010, Mentored-Research has spread its wings into the halls of the premier colleges of India. The education imparted both theoretical and practical, acts as a stepping stone to the financial industry. We carefully nurture the spirit of inquisitiveness and research within the students, encouraging and daring them to reach new height. Our alumni are now highly successful industry professionals, bringing glory to themselves and to Mentored-Research.

Mentored Research is a financial education company, which enables students and working professionals gain actionable knowledge and networking connect; providing them a jumpstart to lucrative careers in finance. Mentored Research has reached and supported more than 1000 candidates till date.

VISION: To be a centre for excellence for practical learning in finance and economics.

MISSION: To enable students and corporates make the career advance to their chosen stream in the field of finance, by providing guided and application oriented courses which leverage technology for easy access.

ABOUT OUR PROGRAM: Each of our programs (Equity Research Initiative, Risk Management Program, Technical Analysis Program) runs twice a year. Programs are provided via the course portal, activated for each registered student. Practical assignments are given to the participants in a staggered fashion, depending on their completion of the previous assignments.
