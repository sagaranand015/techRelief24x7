Equity Research Initiative

Equity Research Initiative, the flagship program of Mentored-Research, is a 6 week course designed to help participants in the art of smart and analytical investing. This is done by dealing with an application-based approach to analyses of securities. In operations since 2010, students from IITs, NITs, BITS, LSR Delhi, Delhi University, Mumbai University, and other colleges of repute have been a part of this program. Students are taught how to analyze businesses and their respective sectors, screen for comparable companies, read financial statements, use ratio analysis techniques, and construct valuation models. With committed mentors to guide them through the program, the students are given dedicated support through carefully designed resource material, in sync with common industry practices.Students are also given the opportunity to interact and network with individuals with industry exposure via interactive webinars/chat sessions.

Equity Research is the process of analyzing a publicly listed company’s financials, performing ratio analysis, forecasting the financials (valuation) and exploring scenarios with an objective of making BUY/SELL stock investment recommendation. Equity Research plays a very critical role that fills the information gap between the buyers and sellers of shares. Also, equity research tries to identify the value stocks out of the massive ocean of stocks and help the buyers to generate profits.

Sample Components

Program Structure

Equity Research Initiative, the flagship program of Mentored-Research, is a 6-week course designed to help participants in the art of smart and logical investing. It is intended to impart equity research training, equipping participants with skills to trade in the stock markets. Through a series of guided assignments involving a real-life company and analysis of real-life data, you are taught how to analyze a business in its respective sector, screen for comparable companies, read financial statements, use ratio analysis techniques, and construct valuation models. At the end of this analysis, a stock pitch is crafted by the participant, summarizing the findings and making an investment recommendation.

Program Support

Participants of ERI are assigned dedicated feedback and communication teams, which help in completing the course smoothly via regular feedback on assignments submitted. In addition, our much-acclaimed query support team is available 24*7 via email to respond to any conceptual or application oriented queries that participants may have. The program also contains other optional components like suggested readings and quizzes, to enable you get a more holistic and detailed perspective of equity research.

Program Differentiators

The application-based approach makes the Equity Research Initiative stand out among other online stock market courses. With committed mentor team to guide through the program, you are given dedicated support through carefully designed resource material, which is regularly kept in sync with common industry practices. Participants are also given the opportunity to interact and network with individuals with industry exposure via interactive webinars/chat sessions.
